# Reference: Marsico / Zentify Music App (Rayhan Mahim)
Source image: 03-music-app-orange-gradient-marsico.png

## What to borrow for Melodia

### Overall Mood
- Deep black background fading to rich orange/red gradient at the edges — cinematic, premium feel
- Strong contrast between the dark bg and the glowing warm tones of album art
- Feels immersive and music-first — the UI recedes and the content (artist/song) is the hero

### Hero / Marketing Composition
- Large bold headline split across two lines: "The future of / music listening / experience" — key words in lighter muted tone, emphasis words in bright white/orange
- CTA buttons: "Create Account" (white filled pill) + "How it Works →" (ghost/text link) — two-tier CTA hierarchy
- Phone mockup floating right, slightly angled — shows app UI in context
- Background typography (large faded wordmark "zentify") adds depth without distracting

### App UI Inside Mockup
- Now playing card: dark surface card, artist name + song title, heart icon
- Progress bar: thin, warm-toned (orange/amber)
- Transport controls minimal and icon-only

### Color Palette to Reference
- Background: pure black #000000 transitioning to #8B1A00 (deep burnt orange) at edges
- Accent / gradient: #FF4500 to #FF8C00 (orange-red range) — maps to Melodia's coral #FF6B4A
- Text: white on dark, muted orange on mid-tones
- The orange gradient is very close to Melodia's coral palette — this whole look is highly transferable

### What This Means for Melodia
- The Lesson Complete / celebration screens could use a similar gradient wash behind the mascot
- Module cards could use a subtle warm gradient overlay on the background photo
- The two-tier CTA pattern (filled pill + ghost text link) matches Melodia's button system exactly
- Paywall screen could use this dark-to-coral gradient for premium feel
