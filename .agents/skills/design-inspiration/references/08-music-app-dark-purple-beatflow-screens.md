## What to borrow for Melodia

### Overall Mood
- Deep dark purple background (#1A0A2E range) — richer and more saturated than Melodia's Midnight (#1A1A2E), but same energy
- Neon green accent (#00FF7F / lime) for primary CTAs — Melodia uses coral instead, but the contrast strategy is identical: one vivid accent on very dark bg
- Gradient overlays on album art cards give depth without complexity

### Home Screen Layout
- Greeting + subtitle at top ("Hello Enamul / Get your earphone ready...")
- "Trending Artists" horizontal scroll with large rectangular cards (artist photo + name + song)
- Play button overlaid directly on card — round white circle with dark play icon
- "Quick Categories" as horizontal pill chips (Songs / Artists / Albums / Genres / Sad Songs)
- "Playlist for you" grid of square art cards

### Now Playing Screen
- Full-screen artist photo as background (blurred/darkened)
- Song title + artist centered below image
- Progress bar: thin, accent-colored scrubber
- Transport controls: shuffle / prev / pause / next / repeat — evenly spaced
- Heart icon top right for save
- "LYRICS" label at bottom with chevron — expandable

### Login / Sign Up Screens
- Minimal: dark bg, app logo centered top, form fields below
- Form fields: dark surface, no visible border (blends into bg), placeholder text only
- Primary button: full-width rounded pill, bright accent color
- Secondary link: plain text, no button treatment

### Colors
- Background: #12052A (very deep purple)
- Surface/cards: semi-transparent dark purple overlays
- Accent: #00FF5E (neon green) — swap for Melodia's coral #FF6B4A
- Text: white primary, #8A8A9A secondary

### Typography
- App name: bold, large, white
- Section headers: medium weight, ~14px, white
- Card labels: small, light weight
- Now playing title: large, bold, centered
